About

At Music Matters Songs, we are more than just a boutique music publishing company — we are dedicated to fostering the success of exceptional songwriters and songs through hands-on management and strategic opportunities.

With our joint venture alongside Sony Music Publishing, we offer both the global reach and personalized attention that ensures our clients thrive.

What sets us apart is our focus on administrative excellence. While it's easy to overlook the small but critical details, we pride ourselves on ensuring that all of our songwriters have their administrative and royalty affairs in order. This precision and attention to detail guarantee that our writers are paid what they deserve, with every opportunity maximized.

We're equally proud of our impressive track record in sync. We've secured high-profile placements with some of the world's leading brands, including but not limited to Samsung, Google, Nike, Superdrug and countless TV shows, showcasing our ability to match music with the right visual projects for maximum impact. Our team's expertise in both administration and sync gives our writers a competitive edge in today's fast-paced music industry.

At Music Matters Songs, we believe that great songs deserve the best possible platform, and we're here to ensure the songs reach their full potential.
