About

Publishing with a long view.

Music Matters Songs is a music publishing company focused on writer development, catalogue stewardship and meaningful creative partnerships built to last.

We work closely with writers and rights holders to build durable value around songs. Our approach is editorial, hands-on and commercially informed, with attention given to both the creative life of the work and the long-term strength of the catalogue.

From early development through to administration, pitching and strategic exploitation, we support songs with care and precision. That means helping writers shape opportunities, protecting rights properly, and creating the conditions for repertoire to travel further across recording, sync, artist and media markets.

We believe strong publishing relationships are built over time. By pairing creative judgement with professional rights management, Music Matters Songs is designed to help writers grow, songs endure and catalogues retain value well beyond the moment of release.
